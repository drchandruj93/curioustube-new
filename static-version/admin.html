How to Add a New Article

To add a new article, copy and paste this template into the articles section of index.html:

<article class="article">
    <div class="article-date">Jan 20 2025</div>
    <div class="article-content">
        <div class="article-preview">
            <p>Your article preview text here...</p>
            <button class="more-btn" onclick="expandArticle(this)">More</button>
        </div>
        <div class="article-full">
            <p>Full article paragraph 1...</p>
            <p>Full article paragraph 2...</p>
            <p>Full article paragraph 3...</p>
            <button class="less-btn" onclick="collapseArticle(this)">Less</button>
        </div>
    </div>
</article>

Email Subscription Setup

Setting Up Email Notifications

Your static website collects email subscriptions, but to send actual emails, you'll need to integrate with an email service. Here are your options:

Option 1: Formspree (Easiest)

Replace the subscription form action with Formspree endpoint:

<form action="https://formspree.io/f/YOUR_FORM_ID" method="POST">

Option 2: Netlify Forms

If you deploy to Netlify instead, add netlify attribute:

<form netlify name="newsletter">

Option 3: ConvertKit/Mailchimp

Replace form with their embed code for professional email management.
